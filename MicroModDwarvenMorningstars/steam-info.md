Praise Armok! (and the many others!)

Unlocks (just) the foreign weapon Morningstars for your Dwarves. Because sometimes you just want your Macelords to have menacing spikes on their majestic steel maces, or your Haunted Embark fort is just begging to become the holiest collection of temples you could think off.

[hr][/hr]
The goal for MicroMods such as this is to add standalone small changes that can easily be toggled on and off, instead of including them in a pack of multiple "All or nothing" features.
